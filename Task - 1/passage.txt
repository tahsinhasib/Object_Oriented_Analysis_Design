In a hotel management system a guest can rent rooms. Hotel receptionist uses
the system to assist in renting. A guest can also book a room for future
renting, with the help of the receptionist but he(R) has to check if the room
has prior booking or not. A guest pays for the rooms at the time they checked
out. He can pay by cash, check or credit card. Receptionist has to login to the
system before they can use it. But to login he has to go through username and 
password verification.
